What is "JSRFMP"?

I'm glad you asked. It's been 20 years since the last time we truly heard from anyone over at SEGA about the future or the Jet Set Series. So, at some point in 2018, someone named Screenracer decided to take things into his own hands and created a fangame called JSRFMP. It's a faithful recreation of Jet Set Radio Future and even adds elements from the original Jet Set Radio. The best part of it? It's a full recreation from scratch and allows online multiplayer without any setup! Kind of a dream for most of us players.

What is "Jet Set Radio"?

Looks like it's a bit of a story time. Way back around the start of the 2000's, the game Jet Set Radio landed on the scene thanks to a company called SEGA. The player controls a member of a gang of graffiti-tagging inline skaters. The game focused on claiming the streets for freedom away from a tyrannical man named Rokkaku Gouji. The game became a cult classic due to the choice of cell-shaded graphics, innovative gameplay, and an incredible soundtrack.

About 2 years later, it's sequel Jet Set Radio Future was released for XBOX and was once again a cult classic. The game was a huge success and the community loved it as much as the original and some people even preferred it over the original. The gameplay carried itself well and the new expansions such as the new designs, graffiti effects, characters and another soundtrack that was a perfect successor to the original made this game what is is.
