How do I submit runs?

Glad you asked! First, you need to make sure that your run is valid. There are two ways to validate your run for submission:
 
For Offline Players:
- be done in devmode;
- have the console open and clearly visible (press the ~ key to the left of the 1 key above the main keyboard);
- have your in-game name typed in the command input field.
 
For Online Players:
- have chatbox not showing any other names beside yours, of course;
- contain two screenshots: one for the time on the win screen, second for player list and your total tag count on the scoreboard (press P key)
 
Once you have this information ping Chroma9 on Discord in the #screenshots channel with this information.

Why are some values "Unknown"?

Before the website existed, only the first three places were documented with the names and times of the runs for Jet Graffiti and Race Mode. "Unknown" is a value for when screenshots could not be validated/found and the owner could not be found/contacted.
